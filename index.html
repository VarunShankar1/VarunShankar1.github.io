Varun Shankar is a sophomore at the University of Maryland pursuing of a B.A. in Journalism and B.S. in Economics. He is the women's basketball beat reporter for Maryland's student newspaper, The Diamondback, and the high school field hockey and softball beat reporter for The Washington Post. Varun will be going to Charlotte this summer to intern with the Charlotte Observer as part of the Sports Journalism Institute.
